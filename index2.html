愛
ài
to love; to tend to
对人或事有深挚的感情
愛 means to love. It is a deep feeling that comes from the heart. Notice the 心 character in the middle.
愛 people Person 1 + 愛 + Person 2
I love my wife. 我愛我的太太。 Wǒ ài wǒ de tàitài.
